2020 Pre-E2M2 Research Workshop

Friday, Jan 3 - Sunday, Jan 5: Mentor Pre-Research Workshop (at Institut Pasteur de Madagascar in Antananarivo)

Friday, Jan 3

  • 8:30-9:00am: Introductions and expectations (Cara)
  • 9:00-10:30am: Former mentor research updates and feedback
    • Each mentor will give a 15 minute powerpoint presentation on their research progress in the past year. Please use the last slide of your presentation to outline any:
      1. roadblocks to your research progress
      2. needs from the E2M2 instructors that would help you progress
  • 10:30-11:00am: Coffee break
  • 11:00am - 12:30pm: One-on-one instructor meetings to set agenda for the pre-workshop
    • Soa Fy meets with Sarah
    • Mirana meets with Tanjona
    • Jean-Marius meet with Cara
  • 12:30 - 1:30pm: Lunch
  • 1:30 - 2:30pm: E2M2 2020 mentors arrive to be briefed on their teaching roles for the rest of the workshop.
  • 2:30 - 5:00pm: Individual former mentor-instructor groups work independently and make progress as needed
  • 2:30 - 5:00pm: R Bootcamp instructors (Fidy and Christian…and Fara?) brief new mentors on their expectations for helping during the Bootcamp and the workshop

Saturday, Jan 4

  • 9:00am - 5:00pm: Independent one-on-one work time between instructors and former mentors
  • Coffee and lunch will be provided throughout the day.

Sunday, Jan 5

  • 8:30am - 3:00pm: Independent one-on-one work time between instructors and former mentors
  • 3:00 - 4:00pm: Former mentors give informal, 10 minute ‘chalk talk’ updates about the past two days of progress. The group brainstorms more effective approaches together.
  • 4:00 - 5:00pm: All instructors available for any last-minute questions.
  • Coffee and lunch will be provided throughout the day.

Monday, Jan 13

  • 8:30am - 5:00pm: A subset of instructors will meet with mentors about progress from the previous week.
  • Coffee and lunch will be provided throughout the day.

Tuesday, Jan 14

  • 8:30am - 5:00pm: Mini-Symposium.
    • Former mentors are invited (but not required) to attend.
